申请信

题干

假定你是李华，暑假在伦敦学习，得知当地美术馆要举办中国话展览。请写一封信申请做志愿者，内容包括：1.写信目的；2.个人优势；3.能做的事情

开头

Dear Sir/Madam,
I’m Li Hua, a student studying in London. I’m delighted to learn that a Chinese painting exhibition will be held in your art gallery. Now I’m writing to apply to be a volunteer.

主体段

结尾

I would appreciate it if you could favorably consider my application. Looking forward to your early reply/response.
Yours,
Li Hua.

邀请信

题干

假定你是李华，计划组织一次郊游，请给你的英国朋友Chris写封邮件邀请他参加。内容包括：1.参加者；2.时间地点；3.活动：登山、野餐等

开头

Dear Chirs,
How is everything going ?/How are you doing ? I’m writing to invite you to join our class in an outing organized by me this Saturday,June 11th.

主体段

结尾

I will be really delighted if you can join us. Looking forward to your reply.
Yours,
Li Hua.

建议信

题干

假定你是李华，你的新西兰朋友Terry将去中国朋友家做客，发邮件向你询问有关习俗。请你回复邮件，内容包括：1.到达时间；2.合适的礼物；3.餐桌礼仪

开头

Dear Terry,
It’a delighted thing to receive your e-mail. Concerning your visit to a Chinese home, I do have some suggestions for you.

主题段

Above all, please bear in mind that you had better arrive on time or some time earlier than the appointed time, which will show your politeness. In addition/Additionally/More over, to be respectful, you might as well bring an appropriate gift, like some fruits. Most importantly, it’s vital that you should mind your table manners. For instance, never speak with your mouth full of food.

结尾

In brief, do as the Chinese do when in China. Wish you great fun at your friend’s home./Hope you can have a pleasant time in your friend’s home.
Yours sincerely,
Li Hua.

通知

题干

你受学生会委托为校宣传栏”英语天地”写一篇通知，请大家观看一部英文短片Growing Together, 内容包括：1.短片内容：学校的发展；2.放映时间、地点；3.欢迎对短片提出意见

开头

Notice
Dear schoolmates,
It’s an honor to inform you on behalf of the Student Union that we’ll invite you to see an English short film named Growing Together, which tells about the history of our school.

主体段

结尾

We welcome/encourage any kind of feedback on the film. Looking forward to your participation.

演讲稿

题干

假定你是李华，下周有新西兰学生访问你校，你将作为学生代表致欢迎辞。请为此写一篇演讲稿，内容包括：1.表示欢迎；2.介绍活动安排；3.表达祝愿

开头

Dear honorable guests, dear teachers and fellow students,
First of all, please allow me to express a warm welcome to our distinguished guests on behalf of the school.

主体段

结尾

Wish you a pleasant time in our school.

新闻稿

题干

假定你是李华，上周日你校举办了5公里越野赛跑活动。请你为学校英文报写一篇报道，内容包括：1.参加人员；2.跑步路线：从校门口到南山脚下；3.活动反响

开头

A Cross-country Running Race
A 5000-meter cross-country running race was held by our school last Sunday.

主体段

结尾

The race turned out to be a great success. Not only does it strengthen our body, but it also tests our will and enriches our life. Every participant expects another race.

议论文

题干

你校将举办英语演讲比赛。请你以Be smart online learners为题写一篇发言稿参赛，内容包括：1.分析优势与不足；2.提出学习建议

开头

Be smart online learners
Online learning is an important way to study recently. But how to become smart learners should become our high priority.

主题段

Undoubtedly/Obviously, online learning may become mainstreaamed for such advantages as easily accessible resources, low cost and great convenience. Besides,current pandemic makes onlinne learning a necessity.
However, long screen time can do much harm to our eyesight, not to mention many online distractions, especially for those lacking self-control.

结尾

Therefore/Thus, we are expected to be smart learners by sensibly/wisely managing our time online. Most importantly, it takes self-discipline to resist all the online distractions towards a smart online learner.

投稿

题干

你校英语报Youth正在庆祝创刊十周年，请你写一篇短文投稿

开头

Learning that Youth is celebrating the 10th anniversary, I’d like to convey my sincere congratulations.

主题段

结尾

I genuinely hope Youth will enjoy more popularity and bring more inspiration to young like me!
